Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you start your athletic journey.

Simply fill out the contact form above with your preferred activity. Our coordinator will contact you within 24 hours to schedule your complimentary assessment session with one of our head coaches.

We have specialized programs for all ages: Grassroots (5-8 yrs), Development (9-13 yrs), Elite Performance (14-19 yrs), and adult weekend clubs.

We provide basic training equipment during sessions. However, for specialized sports like Tennis or Cricket, we recommend players have their own personal kits. Academy jerseys are mandatory for all enrolled members.
