About the Program

Raid with Power & Strategy

Kabaddi demands a unique combination of explosive strength, rapid reflexes, and tactical intelligence. Our program develops raiding techniques, defensive formations, ankle holds, thigh holds, and dash escapes through progressive drill sessions.

From single-leg to chain tackles, every defensive skill is drilled to perfection. Raiders learn touch points, hand touches, toe touches, and running techniques that create scoring opportunities.
