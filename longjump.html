About the Program

Soar Through the Air

Long jump combines raw speed with explosive takeoff power. Our program focuses on the approach run, the precise takeoff point, mid-air technique, and safe landing mechanics.

Athletes learn to convert horizontal velocity into vertical lift, maximizing flight distance through proper body positioning and arm drive.
