FREQUENTLY ASKED

Common Questions

Everything parents and aspiring athletes need to know about joining D.G. Sports Club in Virar.

What sports does D.G. Sports Club offer? expand_more

D.G. Sports Club offers professional coaching in football, athletics (track & field), kabaddi, yoga, fitness training, strength & conditioning, and multi-sport programs for children. We have specialized academies for each sport with certified coaches who have national and state-level experience.

What age groups are accepted? expand_more

We accept students from age 4 to adults of all ages. Our programs are structured by age group: Tiny Tots (4–6 years), Junior (7–12 years), Youth (13–17 years), and Senior (18+ years). Each group receives age-appropriate coaching intensity and curriculum.

Where is D.G. Sports Club located in Virar? expand_more

D.G. Sports Club is located at Main Stadium Complex, Virar East, Maharashtra 401305, India. We have two training locations in Virar serving the wider Vasai-Virar region. Visit our locations page for directions and maps.

What are the coaching hours and schedule? expand_more

Training sessions run Monday through Friday from 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Saturday from 6:00 PM to 7:45 PM, and Sunday sessions from 6:00 AM to 12:00 PM. Morning batches (6–8 AM), afternoon batches (3–5 PM), and evening batches (5–8 PM) are available on weekdays.

How much does membership cost? expand_more

We offer Starter, Pro, and Elite membership tiers at affordable monthly rates. Visit our pricing page for current rates, or contact admissions at +91 82861 32342 for personalized quotes and family discounts.

Is there a free trial session available? expand_more

Yes, D.G. Sports Club offers a free assessment session for all new students. This allows our coaches to evaluate the student's current fitness level, discuss goals, and recommend the most suitable training program. Book your free trial today.

What safety measures are in place for children? expand_more

We prioritize child safety with certified coaches trained in first aid, supervised training environments, proper warm-up and cool-down protocols, age-appropriate equipment, and a secure facility with controlled entry and exit points.

Does D.G. Sports Club prepare students for competitions? expand_more

Yes, we actively prepare students for district, state, and national-level competitions. Our athletes regularly participate in inter-school tournaments, SGFI events, and state championships across football, athletics, and kabaddi.
