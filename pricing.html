Frequently Asked Questions

Are there any hidden charges? expand_more
No. Our pricing is transparent. The only additional fee is a one-time registration charge of ₹1,000 for the Academy ID and insurance, which is waived for Annual Pro members.
What is the age limit for joining? expand_more
D.G. Sports Club offers programs for children from age 6 to seniors up to 65. We have specific age-appropriate batches for all competitive sports to ensure safety and effective learning.
Can I switch plans mid-month? expand_more
Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The difference will be calculated on a pro-rata basis. Plan downgrades are only possible at the end of the current billing cycle.
Do you offer a trial period? expand_more
Absolutely. We offer a free trial session for all new students. Contact us to schedule your complimentary assessment.
